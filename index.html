About AusExperts

AusExperts is a subsurface consulting organization engaged in providing high quality subsurface consulting services to the upstream oil and gas industry. Our consulting services encompass the entire range of subsurface deliverables through exploration, appraisal, development and ongoing reservoir management. We provide services for geology, geophysics, petrophysics, reservoir and petroleum engineering

In this challenging oil price environment what sets us apart from our competition is our ability to provide high quality, yet economical, subsurface consulting services. Our consultants are not only highly experienced, but also experts in using modern, state-of-the-art technology and methods to design solutions and provide answers.
